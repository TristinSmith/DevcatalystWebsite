About Our Establishment

"Here at this restaurant, we want to mix up Mexican and Japanese culture. We also want to make sure you have the best of both foods. It may be tough, but we work our best to make everything awesome!"

From our small family beginning, we have since grown to a fine establishment and are proud to serve all in Ripley and beyond. Our menu, as well, has since grown to provide a heavy variety for people of all tastes.


"I never expected my passion to evolve into something this vast and great. It may have taken years to come to this point, but I have no plans of stopping. I have opened several other Tacos VS Sushi restaurants across West Tennessee. There is a bright future ahead of us."
We clean and disinfect our surfaces regularly and after customer use; the customer's safety is our top priority.
